MAGICOOK.TXT MAGIC COOKIE BARS 1/2 cup margarine 1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs 1 14 oz can sweetened condensed 1 6 oz pk semi sweet chocolate milk chips 1 3 1/2 oz can flaked coconut 1 cup chopped nuts 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees (325 for glass dish). 2. In a 9 X 13 pan, melt margarine in the oven. 3. Sprinkle cracker crumbs evenly over bottom of dish. 4. Pour milk evenly over crumbs. 5. Top with remaining ingredients, press down firmly. 6. Bake 25 - 30 minutes (until lightly browned). 7. Cool completely, cut into bars. Store at room temperature. 
