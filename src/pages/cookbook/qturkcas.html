QTURKCAS.TXT QUICK TURKEY CASSEROLE (Leigha) 2 cups bite size pieces of cooked 3 cups hot cooked brown rice (1 c turkey (or chicken) uncooked) 1 Tbs instant minced onion 1 4oz can mushrooms, drained 1 10 oz can mushroom soup 1/2 tsp poultry seasoning 2 slices whole wheat bread 1 tsp butter 1. Mix turkey, rice, onion, mushrooms, soup, snd poultry seasoning. Pour into a 1 1/2 to 2 quart casserole dish. 2. Combine bread and butter in a blender or food processor. Make into crumbs and sprinkle over the casserole. 3. Cover and bake for 25 - 30 minutes in a 375 degree oven. Serves 6 - 8 
