WHITING.TXT WHITING WITH LEMON 12 whiting fish thawed 3/4 cup butter or margarine, melted 1/3 cup lemon juice 2 tsp salt 2 cloves finely chopped garlic 1 tsp oregano 1/4 tsp pepper 1. Heat the oven to 350 degrees. 2. Grease a baking dish large enough to hold the fish well. Place fish in the dish. 3. Mix remaining ingredients together and pour over the fish. 4. Bake the fish for 25 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork. 
