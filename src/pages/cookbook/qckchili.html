QCKCHILI.TXT QUICK HOMEMADE CHILI 2 Tbs oil 1/2 cup chopped onion 1/2 clove garlic, minced 1/2 medium green pepper, chopped 1 lb ground meat 2 cups canned kidney beans 2 8 oz cans tomato sauce 1 tsp salt 2 tsp chili powder 1. Brown meat, onion, pepper, and garlic in the oil. Drain. 2. Add undrained beans, tomato sauce, salt, and chili powder. 3. Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until chili is at desired thickness - about 45 minutes. 
