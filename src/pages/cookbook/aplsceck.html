APLSCECK.TXT APPLESAUCE COOKIES (Leigha) 1/2 cup oil 3/4 cup honey 1 egg 1 cup applesauce 2 cups whole wheat flour (fresh 1 cup quick oats milled) 1 tsp soda 1 tsp cinnamon 1/2 tsp nutmeg 1 cup chopped dates or raisins 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional) 1. Mix oil, honey, and egg until fluffy. 2. Blend in other ingredients. Mix well. 3. Drop by tablespoonsful on a non-stick cookie sheet. 4. Bake 10 - 12 minutes in a 350 degree oven. Yield 36. 
