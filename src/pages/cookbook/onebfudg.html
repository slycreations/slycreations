ONEBFUDG.TXT ONE BOWL FUDGE 2 pk (8 sq ea) Bakers semi 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed sweet chocolate milk 2 tsp vanilla 1 1/2 cups chopped nuts 1. Microwave chocolate and milk in a large microwaveable bowl on high for 2 - 3 minutes or until chocolate is almost melted, stirring halfway through the heating time. 2. Stir until chocolate is completely melted. 3. Stir in vanilla and nuts. 4. Spread in a greased 8 in square pan. Refrigerate until firm. Cut into squares. 
