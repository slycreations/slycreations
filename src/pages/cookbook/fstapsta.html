FSTAPSTA.TXT LOW FAT FIESTA PASTA (Sherry) 8 oz linguine, cooked 1 lb zucchini, diced 1/4 cup chopped peppers, 1 14 oz can chopped tomatoes, Trappeys or Vlassic drained 1/2 tsp dried oregano 1/3 cup sliced black olives 2 Tbs olive oil 1 med onion, sliced thin 1 clove garlic, crushed 1/2 tsp salt 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms 1. In a skillet, heat oil over medium heat. 2. Saute zucchini, onions, peppers, and garlic for 5 minutes or until tender crisp, stirring occassionally. 3. Add other ingredients except pasta, bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 10 minutes, stirring frequently. 4. Serve over pasta and sprinkle with parmesan if desired. Serves 4. 
