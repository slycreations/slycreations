FRUITSTF.TXT FRUIT STUFF 2 cups flour 1 cup melted margarine 1/2 cup finely chopped pecans 1. Mix well and pat into a 13 X 9 X 2 inch baking dish. 2. Bake for 15 - 20 minutes in a 350 degree oven. Cool completely. FILLING 1 8 oz pk cream cheese 3 cups powdered sugar 2 envelopes dream whip topping 1 can cherry, peach, strawberry, or blueberry pie filling 3. Cream the cream cheese. 4. Add powdered sugar and mix till well blended. 5. Mix dream whip to package directions. Fold into cream cheese mix. 6. Spread over the crust and top with pie filling. 7. Chill overnight before serving. 
