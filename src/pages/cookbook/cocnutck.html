COCNUTCK.TXT COCONUT ICE BOX CAKE (Sharon Buchanan) CAKE 1 Duncan Hines Butter cake mix 1 cup sour cream 1/2 cup oil 1/4 cup sugar 4 eggs 1. Beat together and pour into 3 pans. 2.
