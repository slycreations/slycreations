APLSPTEA.TXT APPLE SPICE TEA (Leigha) 2 qt water 4 tea bags 1 sliced, unpeeled apple 1/4 cup sugar 1/2 tsp cinnamon 2 tsp vanilla 1. Put water in a large saucepan and bring to a boil. 2. Reduce heat. Add teabags, apple, cinnamon, and sugar. 3. Simmer 15 minutes. 4. Remove apple and teabags. 5. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla. 
