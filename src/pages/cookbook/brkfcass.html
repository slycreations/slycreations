BRKFCASS.TXT BREAKFAST CASSEROLE 1 lb sausage 6 eggs beaten 1 cup grated cheddar cheese 2 cups milk 1 tsp salt dash of pepper 4 slices bread 1. The evening before you want to eat this, tear up the bread into small peices and put in a greased 9" X 13" baking dish 2. Brown the sausage, drain well, and spread over the bread. 3. Spread the cheese over the sausage. 4. Add milk, salt, and pepper to eggs. Mix and pour over the cheese. 5. The next morning, bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes. 
