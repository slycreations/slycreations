SPINCHES.TXT SPINACH CHEESE BAKE 1 6 oz pk corn muffin mix 1 10 oz pk frozen chopped sponach, 2 eggs, beaten thawed and drained 8 oz sour cream 1/2 cup margarine 1 10 1/2 oz can french onion 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese soup, undiluted 1. Combine all ingredients except the cheese. Stir until blended. 2. Spoon into a greased 12 x 8 x 2 inch pan. Bake 25 minutes at 350 degrees. 3. Sprinkle the cheese over the spinach mix and bake another 5 minutes until the cheese melts. 
