CHEECHIK.TXT CHEESY BAKED CHICKEN 4 boneless, skinless chicken 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs breast split and flattened Oregano or Italian seasoning 4 slices Provolone cheese Butter or margarine Parsley flakes 1. Press chicken pieces into bread crumbs and then place in a greased baking dish. 2. Sprinkle lightly with italian seasoning (or oregano). 3. Cover each piece with a slice of cheese, a pinch of parsley flakes, and a dot of butter. 4. Bake in a 425 degree oven for about 20 minutes. 
