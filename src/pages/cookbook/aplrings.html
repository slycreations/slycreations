APLRINGS.TXT SAUTEED APPLE RINGS 3 large apples 2 Tbs butter/margarine 2 Tbs brown sugar 3/4 tsp cinnamon 1. Peel, core, and slice the apples. 2. In a skillet, melt butter. Stir in sugar and cinnamon. 3. Add apples, tossing to coat them. 4. Cover and cook over low heat, stirring occasionaly, for 10 - 15 minutes. 
