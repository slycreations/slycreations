HLRCHIKN.TXT HONEY, LEMON, AND ROSEMARY GLAZED CHICKEN (Sherry) 1 cut up chicken 1/3 cup honey 1/4 cup lemon juice 2 tsp dried rosemary, crushed 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper 1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. 2. Combine the honey, lemon juice, rosemary, and pepper in a bowl. 3. Brush on chicken. 4. Bake for 1 hour, brushing occasionally with the glaze. 
