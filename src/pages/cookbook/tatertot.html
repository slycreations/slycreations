TATERTOT.TXT TATER TOT CASSEROLE 2 lb ground beef 1 onion, chopped 2 cans mushroom soup 1 bell pepper, chopped 1 pkg Tater Tots, thawed Salt and pepper to taste 1 cup grated cheddar cheese 1. Saute onion and bell pepper together until tender. 2. Press ground beef into bottom of electric skillet, add salt and pepper, then spread onion mix over the meat. 3. Cover and cook for 5 min at 350 degrees 4. Cover with tater tots, soup, and cheese. 5. Cover and cook until meat is done and cheese is melted. 
