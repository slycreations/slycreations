FRTPIZZA.TXT FRUIT PIZZA (Leigha) 1 pkg pillsbury slice and bake 8 oz cream cheese, softened cookies 1/3 cup sugar 1 Tbs vanilla 1 tsp lemon juice fresh fruit for topping (kiwi, berries, pineapple, seedless grapes, bananas, mandarin oranges, etc.) 1. Slice the cookie dough and space to cover a 12 inch pizza pan. 2. Bake at recommended temperature for 11 to 13 minutes until golden brown. Cool. 3. Mix cream cheese, sugar, vanilla, and lemon juice and pour over the cooled crust. 4. Top with your choice of fruit. 
