CHEESECK.TXT CREAMY BAKED CHEESECAKE 1/4 cup melted margarine 1 14 oz can sweetened condensed milk 1 cup graham cracker crumbs 3 eggs 1/4 cup sugar 1/4 tsp salt 2 8 oz pk cream cheese 1/4 cup lemon juice (reconstituted) softened 1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees. 2. Combine crumbs, sugar, and butter. Mix well. Pat firmly in bottom of 9 inch springform pan. 3. In a large mixer bowl, beat eggs until fluffy. Beat in the milk, cream cheese, and salt until smooth. 4. Mix in the lemon juice and pour into the prepared pan. 5. Bake 50 - 55 minutes or until cake springs back when lightly touched 6. Cool. Top with berries or your favorite topping. 
