PEACHBTY.TXT PEACH BETTY 1 1 lb 13 oz can peach halves 1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed 1 tsp lemon rind 1 - 2 Tbs lemon juice 1/2 tsp mace 3 tbs margarine, melted 3 cups corn flakes 1. Drain peaches, reserving 3/4 cup of the juice. Dice the peaches. 2. Mix juices, sugar, lemon rind, mace, and butter. 3. Add peaches and corn flakes. Mix well and turn into a 1 1/2 quart casserole. Cover. 4. In a 350 degree oven, bake 25 minutes covered. Uncover and bake 15 minutes more. 
