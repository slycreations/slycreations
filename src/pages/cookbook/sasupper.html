SASUPPER.TXT SAN ANTONIO SUPPER (Sherry) 1/2 lb Hilshire Farms Turkey 1 cup enriched white rice Polska Keilbasa 1 14 oz can chopped tomatoes 1 12-16 oz can dark red kidney 2/3 cup Pace medium salsa beans, drained and rinsed 1 cup water 1. Cut sausage lengthwise and then slice. 2. In a skillet sprayed with pam, combine all ingredients, bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes. 3. Remove from heat and let stand 5 minutes. 4. Serve with sour cream, salsa, black olives, or cheese. Serves 4 - 5. 
