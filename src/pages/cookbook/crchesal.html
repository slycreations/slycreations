CRCHESAL.TXT CREAM CHEESE SALAD 2 8 oz pk cream cheese 1 cool whip 1 small can crushed pineapple, 1/2 cup sugar drained 1/2 cup chopped nuts 1 cup minature marshmallows 1. Beat cream cheese, add sugar and beat until smooth. 2. Add pineapple, nuts, and marshmallows. Mix well. 3. Spread in bottom of a large rectangular pyrex baking dish. 4. Top with cool whip, chill and serve. 
