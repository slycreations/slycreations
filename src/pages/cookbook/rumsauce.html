RUMSAUCE.TXT RUM FLAVORED SAUCE 1/4 cup sugar 1 Tbs cornstarch 1/4 tsp nutmeg 3/4 cup water 1 tsp rum extract flavoring 1 Tbs margarine 1. In a saucepan combine sugar, cornstarch, nutmeg, and water. 2. Over medium heat, bring to a boil and boil for 2 minutes, stirring constantly. 3. Remove from heat, mix in the rum flavoring and margarine, stirring until margarine is melted and mixed in. Makes 4 - 6 servings of sauce. Great on bread pudding. 
