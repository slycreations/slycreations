BEEFNOOD.TXT CROCK POT BEEF AND NOODLES 2 lb. lean stew meat 2 envelopes onion soup mix 2 cans mushroom soup 1 lb wide noodles 1 cup water salt and pepper to taste 1. Cut beef into bite size pieces and put in crock pot. 2. Add onion soup mix, mushroom soup, and water. Stir well. 3. Cook on low heat for 8 hours. 4. Cook noodles per package directions. Drain and stir into meat mix and serve. NOTE: You can omit the noodles and serve over rice. Check occasionally to make sure the water stays in and you don't lose the gravy. 
