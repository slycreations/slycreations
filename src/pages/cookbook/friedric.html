FRIEDRIC.TXT FRIED RICE 2 cups cooked rice 2 strips bacon, cut in small pieces 2 eggs soy sauce Green onion, sliced (optional) Water chestnuts (optional) Bamboo shoots (optional) 1. In a skillet, cook bacon until browned. 2. Add rice and optional ingredients. Sprinkle with soy sauce to taste. 3. Cook until hot, stirring constantly. 4. Pile up rice in center of the skillet and break eggs into the rice, stirring constantly until eggs are cooked. 
