BRKPIZZA.TXT BREAKFAST PIZZA 1 lb pork sausage 1 pk (8 oz) refrigerated crescent 1 cup hash brown potatoes rolls thawed 1 cup shredded cheddar, swiss, or 5 eggs monterrey jack cheese 1/4 cup milk 1/2 tsp salt 1/8 tsp pepper 2 Tbs grated parmesan cheese 1. Brown sausage and drain well. 2. Place crescents flat on a 12" pizza pan, points to the center. Press over bottom and up sides to form crust, sealing the perforations. 3. Spread sausage ofer crust, sprinkle with potatoes, then sprrinkle with cheese. 4. Beat eggs, milk, salt, and pepper together and poure over cheese. 5. Sprinkle with parmesan. 6. Bake in a 375 degree oven for 25 - 30 minutes. 
