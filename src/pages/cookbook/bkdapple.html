BKDAPPLE.TXT MICROWAVE BAKED APPLES 6 medium ROME apples 6 Tbs brown sugar washed and cored 3 Tbs butter or margarine 1/2 tsp cinnamon NOTE: When you core the apples, do not go through the bottom. 1. Make a shallow cut in the skin about 1 inch from the bottom of each apple. Put apples in a 12X7 baking dish. 2. Put 1 tbsp brown sugar and 1/2 tbsp butter in the center of each apple. Sprinkle with cinnamon. 3. Cover with waxed paper. Nuke on high for 10 to 14 minutes (until tender). 4. Let stand, covered, for 3 minutes before serving. For 2 apples, use individual custard cups or serving bowls and nuke for 3 - 4 minutes. 
