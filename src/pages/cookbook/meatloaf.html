MEATLOAF.TXT DAD'S OWN MEATLOAF 1 1/2 lb ground chuck 1/2 lb ground pork 3/4 cup oatmeal 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped 1/2 cup celery, finely chopped 1/4 cup bell pepper, finely chopped 1 1/2 tsp salt 1/4 tsp pepper 1/2 tsp garlic powder 3 tsp beef boullion granules 1 egg 1/2 cup catsup NOTE: This recipe works well with 2 lb ground chuck (no pork). 1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. 2. Combine all ingredients and mix thoroughly. 3. Form into a loaf on a large baking pan or dish. 4. Bake 1 hour and 15 minutes. Remove from dish. Let stand 5 minutes before slicing. 
