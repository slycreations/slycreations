SFCRANSL.TXT SUGAR FREE CRANBERRY SALAD (Leigha) 2 pkg cranberries, chopped 4 granny smith apples 1 cup chopped nuts 1 cup granulated sugar substitute 1 large box sugar free rasberry (Nutrasweet spoonful) jello 2 cups hot water 1 cup cold water 1. Disolve Jello in the 2 cups hot water. Stir in the cold water. Chill until thickened. 2. Mix other ingredients together. Stir in the thickened jello. Chill until set. Note: Keeps in frig for a week when covered. 
