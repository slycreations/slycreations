CRESCENT.TXT HAWAIIAN CRESCENTS (Leigha) 1 can crushed pineapple, drained 1 can refrigerated crescent rolls 1 can hormel ham cheese, grated (to taste) 1. Press triangles of crescents together to make rectangles. 2. Mix ham, pineapple, and grated cheese. 3. Place ham mix on a rectangle, cover with another rectangle and press to seal the edges. 4. Bake 15 minutes at recommended temperature. 
