COLESLAW.TXT COLE SLAW 3 cups cabbage, finely chopped. if desired, add 1/4 cup chopped green pepper or 1 cup grated carrot 1. Toss with one of the following dressings: A. mix 3 tbsp sugar, 3 tbsp vinegar, 2 tbsp salad oil, 1 tsp salt; Stir till sugar disolves B. combine 1/3 cup mayo or salad dressing, 1 tbsp vinegar, 2 tsp sugar, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp celery seed; Stir till sugar disolves. 2. Refrigerate for several hours to blend flavors. 
