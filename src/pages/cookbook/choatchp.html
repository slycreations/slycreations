CHOATCHP.TXT QUAKER OAT CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES (Leigha) 1 cup butter, softened 1 1/4 cups brown sugar 1/4 cup granulated sugar 2 eggs 2 Tbs milk 2 tsp vanilla 1 3/4 cup all purpose flour 1 tsp baking soda 1/2 tsp salt 2 1/2 cups oats 1 12oz pkg semi-sweet chocolate 1 cup nuts (optional) chips 1. Heat oven to 375 degrees. 2. Beat butter and sugars until creamy. 3. Add eggs, milk, and vanilla. Beat well. 4. Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix well. 5. Stir in oats, chocolate chips, and nuts, mixing well. 6. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto an ungredased cookie sheet and bake 9 - 10 minutes for chewy, 12 - 13 minutes for crisp cookies. 
