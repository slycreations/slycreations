PNUTBALL.TXT PEANUT BUTTER BALLS 1 stick margarine, softened 3/4 bar parafin wax 1 box confedtioners sugar 2 6oz packages semi-sweet chocolate 1 1/2 cups crunchy peanut or butterscotch morsels butter (or substitute dipping chocolate) 1. Mix margarine, sugar, and peanut butter until it is smooth and creamy. 2. Make into balls (about 2 tsp each). Refrigerate balls for 1 hour. 3. In top of a double boiler, melt wax and morsels (dipping chocolate). 4. Dip balls in the wax mix and put on waxed paper to cool. 
