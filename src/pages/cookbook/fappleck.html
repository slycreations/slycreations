FAPPLECK.TXT FRESH APPLE CAKE 4 cups chopped apples 2 cups sugar 3 cups flour 2 tsp baking soda 1 tsp salt 1 cup oil 2 eggs slightly beaten 2 tsp vanilla 1 cup chopped pecans 1. Put sugar on apples and mix well. 2. Add oil, eggs, and vanilla and mix well. 3. Add dry ingredients, mix well. 4. Pour into greased and floured bundt pan. 5. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour. 
