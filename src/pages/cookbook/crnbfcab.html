CRNBFCAB.TXT CORNED BEEF AND CABBAGE 1 can corned beef 1 medium head cabbage salt and pepper to taste 1 1/2 cups water 1. Shred cabbage by slicing into 1/4 inch cuts. Place in a large skillet. 2. Add the water. 3. Salt well and pepper lightly. 4. Cover and bring to a boil and reduce heat to steam cabbage. After cabbage has cooked for about 10 minutes. Add the corned beef. Stir. 5. Cover and cook until cabbage is tender (about 15 - 20 more minutes). Serve with cornbread. 
