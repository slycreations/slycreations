FRBANANA.TXT FROZEN BANANA SALAD 4 bananas, mashed 1 8 1/2 oz can crushed pineapple 1 Tbs lemon juice 1 6 oz jar cherries, drained and 1/2 cup chopped pecans quartered 3/4 cup sugar 1 tsp salt 1 16 oz carton sour cream 1. Mix all ingredients, stirring well, pour into a 9 in square pan. 2. Freeze until firm. Cut into squares and serve frozen. 
