BASPRKCH.TXT BASIL PORK CHOPS 4 center cut pork chops 1 cup V8 juice 1 tsp basil 1/2 tsp salt 1/4 tsp pepper 2 TBS olive oil 1. Trim excess fat from chops. 2. Brown chops in the oil in a hot large skillet. 3. When chops are browned, add other ingredients. 4. Reduce heat, cover tightly, and simmer 40 minutes (Add water a few tablespoons at a time to prevent burning), turning the chops after 20 minutes. 
