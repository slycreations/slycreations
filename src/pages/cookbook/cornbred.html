CORNBRED.TXT EASY CORN BREAD 1 1/4 cups all purpose flour 3/4 cup corn meal 1/4 - 3/4 cup sugar (to taste) 2 tsp baking powder 1/2 tsp salt 1 cup milk 1/4 cup oil 1 egg, beaten 1. Heat oven to 400 degrees. Grease a 11 X 8 (minimum) baking dish. 2. Combine dry ingredients and mix. 3. Add egg, milk, and oil stirring until the dry ingredients are just moistened. Pour into dish. 4. Bake 20 - 25 min or until light golden brown awnd a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. 
