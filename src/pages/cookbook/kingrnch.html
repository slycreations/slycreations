KINGRNCH.TXT KING RANCH CHICKEN 1 chicken (3# or more) (I generally use a hen) 4 cans mushroom soup 2 cans rotel tomatoes 2 cups grated cheddar cheese 12 oz tortilla chips or 12 corn tortillas torn in 2 inch pieces (the corn tortillas make it more moist) 1. Put chicken in a pot with enough water to cover, salt if desired, cover and simmer until well done. Remove and cool. Shred the meat. 2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. 3. In a large bowl, mix soup and rotel 4. In a buttered 9x13 baking dish, layer 1/3 of the chips (tortillas), 1/3 of the chicken, 1/3 of the cheese, and 1/3 of the soup mix. Repeat layers. 5. Bake 1 hour. Can be served with sliced black olive, chopped tomato, and shredded lettuce. 
