OATRSNCK.TXT OATMEAL AND RAISIN COOKIES 1 cup shortening 2 tsp soda 2 cups sugar 1 tsp salt 4 eggs 2 tsp cinnamon 1/2 cup milk 2 cups seedless raisins 3 cups sifted flour 3 1/3 cups quick cooking oats 1. Mix shortening, sugar, eggs, and milk thoroughly. 2. Blend in flour sifted together with soda, salt, and cinnamon. 3. Stir in raisins and oatmeal. 4. Drop by teaspoonfuls on to greased baking sheet. 5. Bake at 375 degrees for about 12 minutes. 
