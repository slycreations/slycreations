BR3CHPIE.TXT BROCCOLI AND THREE CHEESE PIE 1/2 cup cornmeal 4 eggs, slightly beaten 1/2 cup all purpose flour 1 10 oz pkg frozen chopped broccoli 2 tsp baking powder thawed and drained 1/2 tsp salt 1/2 cup cottage cheese 3 Tbs milk 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese 1 egg, slightly beaten 1 cup shredded monterey jack cheese 1/4 cup finely chopped onion 1/4 tsp salt 1/8 tsp pepper 1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. 2. Combine the cornmeal, flour, baking powder, and salt. 3. Add the milk, 1 egg, and onion. Mix well. 4. Grease a 9 inch pie plate. Pour the flour mix in the pie plate, spreading evenly on bottom and sides. 5. Bake 10 minutes and set aside. 6. Combine the 4 eggs, salt, pepper, broccoli, and cheeses. Spoon evenly over the crust. 7. Bake 45 - 50 minutes, until a knife inserted in center comes out clean. 8. Let stand 5 minutes before serving. Serves 6. (23 gr carbo, 21 gr protein) 
